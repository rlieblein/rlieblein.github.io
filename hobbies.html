About Me

I am a senior at the University of Florida studying computer science engineering. I found out I was passionate about computer science as a whole during my senior year of high school. I took an AP computer science class my senior year and my eyes were open to the vastness of the computer world. That was when I decided to study computer science with a minor in sales engineering.

I love to play soccer, travel, go to the beach, watch sports, and spend time with my family and friends. I currently intern with Mindtree Ltd. making web applications. Hopefully, one day, I will find a job that allows me to work and travel simultaneously. I am teaching myself computer languages like MongoDB, Node.js, and PHP in addition to the ones that I have experience with (can be found on my resume) and I am also learning to become fluent in German and Spanish.
